    About me

    Anil Thapa

    Anil Thapa was born on 30th Srawan 2046 to father, Lava Bahadur Thapa and mother, Manju Thapa in Phutung, Kathmandu. As for his education and schooling he started his education at School: Bright Himrashmi Boarding School located in Sangle Khola, Kathmandu until 5th grade and then went to Shree Sangla Balkumari Ma Vi and joined in 6th grade and completed his SLC there. Later he pursued his +2 from NR College located in Manamaiju. After completing his +2 Anil Thapa started his own hardware business which is located in manmaiju. He also took part in some training at that same period of time: He took acting training at M Art Theatre located in Baneshwor with Birenndra Hamal for a whole year along with dance training at G1 Creation located in Bagbazar with Naresh Bir Singh for nearly a year to make sure that those talents would make him stand out and not become a weak point in his further career. Before starting acting training, Anil Thapa did a photo shoot and submitted those pictures to M Art Theatre. After some time a Nepali director Sunil Pandey noticed his photos and decided to audition him and later ended up selecting him for his upcoming movie at that time. Finally he debut his filmy career as an actor in the movie titled ‘Hello Prakriti’ opposite to Sumina Ghimire.

    Later Anil Thapa got offers to seen in some paper advertisement like Ncell Dish Home, and Mahindra Scooty. After some paper ads, some popular company gave him a chance to feature in TV commercial advertisement as well like Gold Mart, Pepsi etc. Anil Thapa did many catwalks in various fashion shows like: SABHA Nepal Fashion Shows, Ncell fashion shows, TGIF Nepal Fashion Shows and many more during the year 2011 and 2012. He also participated in the MR Rajdhani in 2010, reached top 10 finalists and managed to achieve the brilliant ‘Mr Handsome Award’. His friend Benisha Hamal introduced him with Prasna Poudel who gave him a chance to feature in music video as a model. Anil Thapa finally debuted in a music video titled ‘Age Age TopaiKko Gola’ by Pradesh Limbu. Later he was introduced with the amazing director Alok Nembang who then gave him an opportunity to feature in the music video titled ‘Dubi Jane Gham Ho’ by Sharmila Bardewa. After that he contined to feature in Alok Nembang’s music videos and has done more than 20 songs with Alok nembang. Anil also appeared in a song titled Timi Bahek which was the most popular Indian play back singer (Shreya Ghosal.)
